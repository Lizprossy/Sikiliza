WHO WE ARE

We are an innovative start-up, highly specialised in fieldwork management. We offer a complete range of services in the field of research with a high level of expertise and experience in the Agriculture, Public health and Education sectors.

Our mission is to innovate in the sector by introducing new tools and methods of managing field work, combining our expertise in IT and Economics with the passion and experience of over 10 years of datacollection activities.

Our strengths

The Team

Our team is extremely dynamic and flexible, ready to deal with any new challenge. We have an extensive network of highly-qualified professionals, with solid know-how in individual research areas and the various methodologies specific to the sector.

Efficiency

Our step-by-step planning approach, experience and flexibility make us the ideal partner. Guaranteed results from a reliable team with a strong focus on punctuality and quality. We rely on a widespread field network throughout Uganda: area supervisors, quantitative and qualitative interviewers, researchers, and translators.

Quality

We follow the strictest quality standards, industry guidelines and codes of conduct: MAKSS-REC, MUREC.

Expertise

Staff with a high level of expertise and soft skills, a love of teamwork and a commitment to continuing professional development.

Innovation

New fieldwork management tools enable us to deliver service of the highest level.

Support

Who we are

Services

Fieldwork

We work alongside research institutes and agencies in the total management of the operational phase of Research.

Consultancy

We support clients in the research design process to identify the best solutions together.

Full service

We support our clients throughout all research phases: planning, data collection, processing and the presentation of results.

The main services we offer in the Agriculture, Public health and Education sectors are as follows:

  • Project management
  • Carrying out quantitative interviews (face-to-face, online, telephone, at home, in-hall)
  • Carrying out qualitative interviews (in-depth face-to-face, on web platform, by phone, focus groups in-person or on an online platform), also in English
  • Respondent recruitment with or without a list of contacts
  • Desk research
  • Scripting and hosting of CAWI,CAPI and CATI questionnaires
  • Editing, coding
  • Transcriptions in English
  • Translation of materials
  • Data processing
  • Analyses and reports in English
  • Dashboard management
  • Management of respondent incentives
  • Management of adverse events

Tools and sectors of operation

Through the experience built up over time Sikiliza has the capacity to operate in various sectors, drawing upon an in-depth knowledge of the specific characteristics of each market:

  • Agriculture
  • Public Health
  • Education
  • Livelihoods
  • Climate change and Renewable energy
  • Gender

We use rigorous techniques to carry out studies on concepts, behavioural trends, social issues, respondent journeys.

Our fieldwork systems include:

  • CATI, CAWI, CAPI on the SurveyCto platform
  • F2F interviews, Focus Groups and IDIs in-person and online using the 3CX communication system
  • Data collection from social media and the web

We are currently developing new, innovative proprietary tools for use in the provision of fieldwork services, through partnerships with IT companies.

Services

Experience

Operating in research requires the ability to establish an ongoing relationship of trust with all stakeholders within the respective sectors: Research ethics bodies, Universities, Government,participant associations and the respondents themselves.

Our entire team's knowledge, previous experience and know-how within research enables us to provide valuable support in any type of field related research.

Tools and services

We are able to collect data and produce detailed output using classic, sector-specific methodologies and also using innovative research systems.

We are innovating proprietary software for profiling Data collection and Research professionals in both the public and private sectors: all selectable by specialisation, experience and geographical area.

With the collaboration of research ethics bodies and local government authourities, we are also able to contact the respondents themselves, including those in hard to reach areas.

All services offered by Sikiliza are available for research in Uganda.

Processes

Our team keeps constantly up-to-date with the training offered by higher learning institutions for the continuing professional development of everyone involved in the research. We ensure the effective management of adverse events, with punctual and detailed reporting, according to the time frames and methods specified by our clients under the guidelines set by respective IRB.

The services provided by Sikiliza comply with the guidelines of MAKSS-REC (Makerere School of Social Sciences Research Ethics Committee), MUREC (Mildmay Uganda Research Ethics Committee), and the Ministry of ICT .

The highly sensitive and critical nature of the data collected and processed leads us to prioritise the requirements of the GDPR using procedures that are fully integrated into every step of the activities involved.
